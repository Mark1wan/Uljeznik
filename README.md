# ULJEZNIK

A Dubrovnikan take on the social impostor game — runs entirely offline from your phone, perfect for groups of 3 or more.

**"Uljeznik"** is Croatian for *intruder* or *impostor*.

## What is it?

Think *Among Us* or *Town of Salem*, but stripped back to a simple social game you can play anywhere. Every round, all players share a secret word — except the impostor, who is left completely in the dark. The impostor's goal is to blend in, pick up clues from the conversation, and figure out what the word is without getting caught. Everyone else is trying to expose them before they do.

What makes Uljeznik unique is its Dubrovnikan twist: the word lists draw from the Dubrovnik dialect and local vocabulary, so while the game is fully playable in English, it doubles as a fun way for everyone at the table to pick up some of the language.

## How to play

1. Open `Uljeznik_V156.html` in any browser — no installation needed.
2. Enter the names of all players (3 minimum).
3. Choose how many impostors there are.
4. Pass the phone around — each player taps to privately see their hint.
5. Everyone who isn't the impostor gets a clue about the secret word. The impostor gets nothing.
6. Discuss, ask questions, bluff, and vote — flush out the intruder before they figure out the word.

## Features

- **Offline** — everything is embedded in a single HTML file, no internet required after the page loads.
- **Bilingual** — fully playable in Croatian or English, with Dubrovnik dialect vocabulary throughout.
- **No install** — open the file in a browser and play. Nothing to download, no accounts, no setup.
- **Mobile-first** — designed to be passed around between players on a phone.
- **Configurable** — set the number of impostors and choose word category presets.

## Running the game

Double-click `Uljeznik_V156.html` or drag it into a browser tab. That's it.
